How to use screenshare/cam

A minimalistic tutorial for Study Together!

1. Join the screenshare/cam-study call in the server

2. Once in-call, click on 'screen-cam-group-chat'

3. Click on the link above

4 Click on the chat again, 'screen-cam-group-chat'.

5 If it's right you should see a pop-up on your screen. Hover over it, click on the screenshare button.

Happy studying!
